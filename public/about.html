About Pastafarianism

Pastafarianism, also known as the Church of the Flying Spaghetti Monster, is a social movement that promotes a light-hearted view of religion and opposes the teaching of intelligent design and creationism in public schools.

The Flying Spaghetti Monster is the deity of the Church of the Flying Spaghetti Monster. The importance of meatballs in this religion is a symbol of the earth and all its inhabitants, reminding us of our interconnectedness and shared origins.

In Pastafarianism, spaghetti is revered as the divine meal of the Flying Spaghetti Monster. It is believed that each strand of spaghetti is a testament to the complexity and interconnectedness of the universe, and consuming it is a sacred act of communion with the deity.

Pastafarians often express their faith by wearing a pasta strainer on their heads, especially when taking photos for official documents such as driver's licenses. This act is seen as a symbol of their commitment to the teachings of the Flying Spaghetti Monster and their rejection of mainstream religious dogma.
